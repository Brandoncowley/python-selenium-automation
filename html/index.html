How To Make An Espresso Martini

Ingredients:

  • 2 ounces vodka
  • 1/2 ounce coffee liqueur (usually Kahl√∫a)
  • 1 ounce espresso, freshly brewed (or cold brew concentrate)
  • 1/4 ounce simple syrup
  • Strainer
  • Ice
  • Coffee beans (as garnish)
  • Martini glass
  • Cocktail shaker

Instructions:

  1. Add vodka, coffee liqueur, espresso and simple syrup to a shaker filled with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  2. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with 3 coffee beans.
  4. Enjoy your evening with a classy nightcap.
